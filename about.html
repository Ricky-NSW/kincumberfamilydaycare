About Kincumber Family Day Care

As a Jellybeans educator, they have ensured I am trained in, or are obtaining, Certificate III in Children's Services. This specialist childcare knowledge, along with Jellybeans on-going training and support, ensures your child is in the best hands. Jellybeans educators are insured, registered and have passed a working with childrens check. Jellybeans ensure that every adult who resides in the home has passed a working with childrens background check. Children are educated and supported through play meeting their needs and interests through the Early Years Learning Framework.

Gillian Hobbs- Jellybeans Family Daycare, Kincumber educator.

As an educator with a Bachelor Degree in teaching and Certificate III in early childhood, I am able to combine my experience and knowledge to provide programs that meet your child's needs and work towards their developmental milestones.

To work towards meeting developmental milestones, I promote and utilise the outcomes associated with the Early Years Learning Framework which directs your child to where they need to be before entering the school years.

I am a mother of two young children and understand the need for compassionate care in a welcoming and friendly environment for your children.

Within the family daycare service, we maintain low ratios which gives your child the care and attention they are entitled to.
